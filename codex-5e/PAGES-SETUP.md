# GitHub Pages — two phone apps

Both apps are hosted from this repo on **separate URLs**:

| App | Phone URL |
|---|---|
| **Hub (pick an app)** | https://dralorex.github.io/General-Questions/ |
| **Codex** (5e sheet) | https://dralorex.github.io/General-Questions/codex/ |
| **Aincrad Mana** | https://dralorex.github.io/General-Questions/mana/ |

## Install on your phone

1. Open the Codex or Mana URL above in your phone browser.
2. **iPhone (Safari):** Share → **Add to Home Screen**
3. **Android (Chrome):** menu → **Install app** / **Add to Home screen**

Do that once per app if you want **two icons**. Saves stay on that phone.

## Auto-deploy

Pushes to `main` that change `codex-5e/`, `kirito-mana-app/`, or `pages-hub/` redeploy via `.github/workflows/deploy-codex.yml`.
