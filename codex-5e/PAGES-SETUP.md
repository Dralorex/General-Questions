## Install on your phone

1. Open the Codex URL above in your phone browser.
2. **iPhone (Safari):** Share → **Add to Home Screen**
3. **Android (Chrome):** menu → **Install app** / **Add to Home screen**

It works offline after the first load. Your save stays on that phone.

## Auto-deploy

Pushes to `main` that change `codex-5e/` redeploy this site via `.github/workflows/deploy-codex.yml`.
